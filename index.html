    With a vision of becoming a leading manufacturer of folding French doors, windows, steel doors, louvers and safety doors in India, Hi Tek Industries was established in 1995 in Pune. We at Hi Tek Industries offers the best possible quality and a wide variety of products. Hi Tek Industries has an unparalleled variety of products which are customised according to the customers' expectations and imaginations. Hi Tek Industries has an outstanding reputation for having one of the best and advanced machinery setups in the field. With 2 production units of around 8000 sq. ft each, situated in Pune has one of the best production rates in the field.

    Hi Tek Industries also has a 3000 sq. ft showroom which also happens to be the first showroom totally dedicated to folding French doors and windows, louvers, steel doors and safety doors. Our R&D team which we proudly call vision team is totally involved in making our products more efficient to use and the products are precisely checked for its quality at every stage of the production. At Hi Tek Industries manufacturing unit each and every stage is accompanied by an engineering drawing without which the cycle is not complete. We have best qualified staff for both the stages-corporate and production. The staff is totally dedicated to the customer satisfaction and maintains a good after-sales relation.
